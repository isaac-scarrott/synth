Countdown bar. Only self-dismissing toasts (done) carry it: 2px, --focus (charcoal / champagne), ends inset by the card radius with soft caps — the split focus bar's exact grammar, reused as a clock. It drains toward its left origin; dismissal fires on the bar's animationend, so bar and timer can never disagree.
